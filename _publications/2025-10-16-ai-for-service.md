We propose Alpha-Service, a proactive assistance system with AI glasses. By integrating multimodal perception with agentic decision-making, Alpha-Service delivers real-time, context-aware assistance in everyday scenarios. The system leverages continuous visual and auditory sensing to understand user context, anticipate needs, and proactively offer relevant services through a natural conversational interface.
